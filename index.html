How it works

Seven steps. Zero awkward asks.

Coffee. Gym. Sunset walk. Whatever you actually want to do — just post it.

Step 3

You see their vibe — activities, energy, compatibility score. No name. No photo. No profile.

Step 6

They confirm

Both sides have to say yes. Nothing is revealed until you both mean it.

Names reveal
