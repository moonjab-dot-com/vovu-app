Terms of Use

Last updated: January 2026

1. .edu verification required

You must be a currently enrolled student with a valid .edu email address to use Vovu. Sharing or misusing another person's credentials is prohibited and will result in immediate account termination.

2. No harassment

You agree not to use Vovu to harass, intimidate, or harm any other user. Any misuse of the platform — including using match information to stalk or contact someone who has not consented — will result in permanent account termination and may be reported to campus authorities.

3. Plans expire automatically

All plans expire within 8 hours of creation. Vovu does not maintain a history of your past plans or matches. Once a plan expires or a match concludes, the connection data is removed from active records.

4. Last names are never shared

This is both a product promise and a legal commitment. Vovu will never share your last name with any other user, under any circumstances. The only information revealed after a mutual yes is your first name and your campus email address.

5. In-person meetings

Vovu facilitates connections between students but is not responsible for what happens in person. Meet in public campus spaces. Trust your instincts. If something feels wrong, don't go.

6. Contact

Questions about these terms? Email us at hello@vovu.co. We respond within 48 hours.
