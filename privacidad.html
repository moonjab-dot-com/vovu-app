Privacy

Last updated: January 2026

What we collect

  • · Your campus email address (.edu only)
  • · Your campus (derived from email domain)
  • · Profile answers from onboarding (activities, vibe, preferences)
  • · Plans you post (general area, time window, note)
  • · Plans you apply to (never disclosed to the poster before a match)

What we never collect

  • · Your full name (first name only, from your email)
  • · Your phone number
  • · Your GPS or precise location data
  • · Photos or profile pictures
  • · Payment or financial information

What we never share

  • · Your last name — ever, to anyone
  • · Your exact location before a match is confirmed
  • · Your identity as an applicant before a mutual yes
  • · Your data to third-party advertisers

How data is stored

Vovu uses Supabase (built on PostgreSQL) hosted on AWS. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Authentication uses magic links — no passwords are stored. Plans expire automatically and are not kept as historical records.

Your rights

You can request full deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Email us at hello@vovu.co — we'll handle it within 48 hours, no questions asked.
