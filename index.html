How it works

Step 1

Post a plan

Coffee tomorrow afternoon. Gym after class. Whatever you actually want to do.

Step 2

Stay vague in public

Only the general area and time window are visible. The exact spot stays private.

Step 3

People apply anonymously

You see their vibe — activities, energy, compatibility score. No name, no photo.

Step 4

Check the match score

Four categories scored before you ever say a word to each other.

Step 5

You pick someone

Send a yes to whoever feels right. No pressure, no awkward DM.

Step 6

They confirm

Both sides have to say yes. Nothing happens until you both mean it.

Step 7

The reveal

First names, campus emails, the exact spot and time. Last names — never.
