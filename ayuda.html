Frequently asked questions about how Vovu works.

How does Vovu work?

You post a plan — gym at 4pm, coffee at 3pm, anything. Others on your campus apply anonymously. You see their vibe: activity tags and two bars (how often they follow through, how open they are to meeting new people). You pick someone. They say yes back. Only then do you see each other's first name and campus email.

Is my full name shared?

Never. Vovu only reveals your first name after a mutual yes. Your last name is never shared. By anyone. This is a product promise and a legal commitment.

What if I change my mind after applying?

You can withdraw your application at any time before the plan creator picks someone. No explanation needed. No awkward follow-up.

My exact location — when does it get shared?

Only after both parties say yes. The feed shows only a general area (e.g. "near village") and a time window (e.g. "afternoon"). The exact spot and exact time are revealed to both people simultaneously when the match is confirmed.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at hello@vovu.co and we'll handle it within 48 hours. We delete everything: your profile, your plans, your application history.

Which campuses are on Vovu?

Vovu launches campus by campus. Starting at Kenyon College. More campuses announced soon. If you want Vovu at your school, join the waitlist.
