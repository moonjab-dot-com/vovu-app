What we collect

Your .edu email address (for verification and magic link auth), your first name, your campus, and your activity and personality preferences from onboarding. We do not collect your last name, phone number, photo, or location.

How we use it

To verify you are a current student at your campus, to calculate compatibility scores between users, to send you magic link emails, and to reveal your first name and campus email only after a mutual match.

Third parties

We use Supabase for database hosting (your data lives on their servers), Resend for sending magic link emails, and GitHub Pages for hosting the app. None of these parties receive your data for advertising purposes.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us. Under GDPR and CCPA you have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data.
